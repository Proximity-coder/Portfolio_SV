Full lifecycle, every time.

My role covered the full event lifecycle — from early planning and logistics to onsite delivery and post-event reporting. I worked across all service areas: live and hybrid meetings, CME-accredited courses, congress management, and association membership services.

  • Membership management and CRM for non-profit medical societies
  • Budget support and sponsor liaison
  • Event marketing & branding: graphic design, web, email campaigns, social media
  • Logistics coordination for speakers, faculty, attendees and sponsors
  • Vendor relations, contract negotiation and PO processing
