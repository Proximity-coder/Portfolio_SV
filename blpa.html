BLPA Worldwide Hockey Tours

The Beer League Players Association (BLPA) is an international community of adult recreational hockey players. In 2025 I was brought on as a freelance Event & Tour Manager to manage their Worldwide Hockey Tours — an ongoing series of approximately 12 tours per year across destinations in Europe, North America and South America.

The project

The BLPA Worldwide Hockey Tours bring together recreational hockey players for multi-day trips combining competitive games against local teams with cultural and travel experiences. Each tour is a full event in its own right — requiring careful itinerary design, logistics, communications and branding.

I am responsible for the full lifecycle of this ongoing project, from initial concept and tour design through to participant communications, logistics coordination and post-tour reporting.

What I'm working on
  • Tour itinerary design across multiple destinations
  • Website design and development
  • Brand identity and visual communication
  • Participant communications and community management
  • Logistics coordination with local partners and venues
  • Travel and accommodation management
  • Event programming and schedule design
  • Post-tour reporting and participant feedback
