Cookie Policy.

Last updated: April 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how sofiavicinanza.com uses cookies and similar technologies. This policy should be read alongside my Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work correctly, to improve user experience, and to provide information to website owners.

2. Does this website use cookies?

This website is a static portfolio site hosted on GitHub Pages. It does not use:

  • Analytics or tracking cookies (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Advertising or marketing cookies
  • Social media tracking cookies
  • Session cookies for user logins

3. Third-party cookies

While I do not set cookies directly, certain third-party services used by this website may set cookies on your device:

GitHub Pages (hosting)

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc. / Microsoft). GitHub may use cookies as part of their infrastructure and security services. These are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be disabled. For details, see GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Google Fonts & Fontshare

This website loads fonts from Google Fonts and Fontshare. When your browser requests these fonts, the providers may log the request including your IP address and browser information. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Web3Forms (contact form)

The contact form on this site is powered by Web3Forms, which processes your submission to deliver the message via email. Web3Forms may set functional cookies as part of this service. For details, see the Web3Forms Privacy Policy.

4. How to control cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some websites. The following links explain how to manage cookies in major browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

You can also opt out of interest-based advertising through Your Online Choices (EU).

5. Changes to this policy

I may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
